Change Log

Check out the changes listed below to stay up to date on whats been added, removed, and/or fixed.

March 10th 2022

Added

Combat Mechanics

  • Players can now attack by clicking the left mouse
  • Attacks can damage enemies and chests
  • Plasma balls from drones can now damage the player
  • Player health is now displayed in the top left HUD

Enemies

  • Gaurd enemies now spawn in dungeon biomes

Biomes

  • Biomes now supported
  • Biomes specific a level's tileset, enemy spawns, and background color
  • The Dungeon biome now spawns drones and gaurds
  • The Cave biome now spawn only bats

Sound Effects

  • Jumping plays a sound which increases in pitch each consecutive jump without landing
  • Attacking plays a swipe sound selected randomly from a list of over 10 variations

Removed

Looks like nothing got removed this week

Fixed

Player Movement

  • Player no longer gets stuck on some corners

Graphics

  • Removed black border from the drone enemy sprites
  • Adjusted some cave tiles to provide a more cohesive tileset
